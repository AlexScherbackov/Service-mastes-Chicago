Commercial Mold Removal Services

ServiceMaster Restoration By Simons provides mold remediation and mold damage cleanup in Chicago, IL and surrounding areas.

ServiceMaster Restoration by Simons specializes in mold inspection, mold remediation, and mold cleanup in commercial buildings. We also provide residential mold removal for Chicago homes.

Mold, which can grow in as little as two days any time excessive moisture is present, is a major concern for commercial enterprises, insurance providers, and restoration companies. Indoor mold contamination can cause structural damage and has been identified as a contributor to allergies, illnesses, and poor air quality.

Because of the special challenges that mold contamination presents, our experts will work quickly to address the mold problems in your commercial property and take immediate steps to remedy them. Our goal is to work with you and your insurance company to establish the proper procedures to handle mold inspection, remediation, and cleanup.

  • Step 1:Apply solution to loosen packed-in dirt and debris.
  • Step 1:Rinse with clear, hot water using industry grade equipment.
  • Step 1:Extract all water, cleaning solutions, and dirt.
  • Step 1:Groom carpet for a unified appearance and place pads under furniture while it dries.
  • Step 6: Final walk through to guarantee your complete satisfaction.

Carpet cleaning, when compared to the cost of replacement, is an effective and efficient use of your money. If your carpet has been stained from soot after a fire or water from a flooded basement, there’s no need to panic. Contact ServiceMaster Restoration by Simons.
